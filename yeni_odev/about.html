our story
how it all began

Bennett Tea was founded in 2017 by a team of successful entrepreneurs and leaders in the food and tea industry with the common goal to advocate for health and wellness.

Our team includes North America’s most awarded in-house Tea Master with over 12 years of blending experience who has been working globally with the world’s finest tea producers to develop a masterfully created line of superior quality loose leaf teas that are all 100% organic, and free of toxic chemicals and additives.
